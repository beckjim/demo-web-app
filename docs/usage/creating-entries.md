# Creating Entries

Guide for employees to create self-assessment entries.

## Overview

The self-assessment form allows employees to evaluate their own performance across multiple dimensions:

- Objective achievement
- Technical abilities
- Project management
- Methodology
- Efficiency and collaboration
- Professional conduct

## How to Create an Entry

### Step 1: Sign In

1. Navigate to the application homepage
2. Click **Sign in with Microsoft**
3. Authenticate with your Microsoft 365 account

### Step 2: Start a New Entry

- If you already have an entry, you'll be directed to edit it
- Otherwise, click **Create New Entry** to start your self-assessment

### Step 5: Efficiency & Collaboration

Evaluate your:
- **Collaboration** - Working with teammates
- **Ownership** - Taking responsibility
- **Resourcefulness** - Problem-solving and creativity

Provide an **Efficiency Comment** (required)

### Step 6: Professional Conduct

Rate your:
- **Mutual Trust** - Building relationships
- **Proactivity** - Taking initiative
- **Leadership** - Influencing and guiding others

Provide a **Conduct Comment** (required)

### Step 7: General Comments

Add any additional feedback or context (required)

**Feedback Received** - Have you received feedback from your manager? (Yes/No)

### Step 8: Submit

Click **Submit** to save your self-assessment.

## After Submission

Once submitted:
- Your manager will receive a notification
- Your entry will appear in your assessment history
- You can edit your entry until a final assessment is created
- Your manager will provide feedback in a separate final assessment

## Editing Your Entry

Before your manager creates a final assessment:

1. Click **Edit** on your entry
2. Update any information
3. Click **Save Changes**

!!! warning
    Once a final assessment is created, you cannot edit your self-assessment.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I save my entry as a draft?**
A: No, all fields must be completed. However, you can edit your entry anytime until a final assessment is created.

**Q: Who sees my self-assessment?**
A: Only your manager and system administrators have access.

**Q: Can I delete my entry?**
A: Yes, click **Delete** before your manager creates a final assessment.

**Q: What if I disagree with my manager's feedback?**
A: Contact your HR representative or manager directly for discussion.
