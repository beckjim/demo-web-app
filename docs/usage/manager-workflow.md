# Manager Workflow

Guide for managers reviewing and providing feedback on employee assessments.

## Manager Dashboard

Managers see two sections:

### 1. Employee Self-Assessments Pending Review

Lists all employee assessments awaiting manager feedback:
- Employee name
- Assessment date
- Status: **Pending Review**
- Action: **Review & Create Final Assessment**

### 2. Final Assessments in Progress

Lists final assessments you're currently working on:
- Employee name
- Employee self-assessment (reference)
- Your feedback status
- Action: **Continue Editing**

## Reviewing a Self-Assessment

### Step 1: Open the Assessment

### Step 2: Read Carefully

- Review each section thoroughly
- Look for alignment between ratings and comments
- Consider context of employee's role and goals
- Note any areas of concern or excellence

### Step 3: Create Final Assessment

Click **Create Final Assessment** to:
1. Lock the employee's self-assessment (read-only)
2. Open your feedback form
3. Start providing managerial feedback

## Providing Feedback

### Final Assessment Form

You have read-only access to the employee's self-assessment and can provide:

#### Objective Feedback
- Review their objective rating
- Provide your management comment
- Adjust goals if needed

#### Abilities Feedback
- Comment on technical competencies
- Provide guidance for improvement
- Recognize strengths

#### Efficiency Feedback
- Evaluate collaboration and teamwork
- Comment on ownership and proactivity
- Discuss resourcefulness

#### Goals for Next Period
- Set SMART goals for next review cycle
- Align with organizational objectives
- Discuss career development

#### General Comments
- Overall performance summary
- Strengths to continue
- Areas for development
- Next steps and expectations

## Completing the Final Assessment

### Before Submitting

1. Review all your comments for clarity
2. Ensure alignment with employee's self-assessment
3. Double-check goals are realistic and measurable
4. Verify tone is professional and constructive

### Submitting

Click **Submit Final Assessment** to:
- Save all feedback
- Notify employee
- Lock both assessments
- End current review cycle

!!! note
    Once submitted, the assessment is locked. Contact HR if corrections are needed.

## Timeline and Deadlines

| Phase                  | Timeline  | Action                         |
| ---------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------ |
| Self-Assessment Period | 1-2 weeks | Employees complete assessments |
| Manager Review         | 5-7 days  | Managers review                |
| Feedback Period        | 3-5 days  | Managers provide feedback      |
| Discussion (Optional)  | 1 week    | Manager-employee discussions   |

- Employees see only their own assessments

## Troubleshooting

### Employee hasn't submitted assessment

- Send reminder via system
- Follow up directly
- Document non-submission

### Can't create final assessment

- Employee may not have submitted yet
- One final assessment already exists
- Contact system administrator

### Need to edit submitted assessment

- Contact HR department
- Document reason for change
- Provide updated assessment
