# Managing Entries

Manage your self-assessment entries and track their status.

## Viewing Your Entries

### Dashboard

The main dashboard shows:

1. **Your Assessment** - Your current or most recent self-assessment
2. **Status** - Whether it's pending review, under final review, or completed
3. **Action Buttons** - Edit, delete, or view options

### Entry List

Click **View All** to see:
- All your self-assessments
- Creation and update dates
- Current status
- Associated final assessments

## Editing Your Entry

### Before Final Assessment

Your entry can be edited anytime before your manager creates a final assessment.

**To edit:**
1. Click **Edit** on your entry
2. Update the information
3. Click **Save Changes**

### What You Can Modify

- Any rating (Objective, Technical, Project, etc.)
- Any comment or description
- Feedback received status

!!! note
    Changes are saved immediately. Your manager will see the updated version.

## Deleting Your Entry

### Requirements

- No final assessment has been created yet
- You are the entry owner

### How to Delete

1. Click **Delete** on your entry
2. Confirm the deletion

!!! warning
    Deleted entries cannot be recovered. All data will be permanently removed.

## Viewing Linked Final Assessments

1. It will appear on your dashboard
2. Click **View Final Assessment** to see:
   - Your original self-assessment
   - Your manager's feedback

## Feedback Timeline

1. **You submit** your self-assessment
2. **Manager reviews** within 5-7 business days
3. **Manager provides** feedback and creates final assessment
4. **You receive** notification of final assessment
5. **Discussion** (optional) with your manager

## Troubleshooting

### Can't edit my entry

- Ensure you're the entry owner
- Check if a final assessment has been created
- Contact your manager if unsure

### Entry disappeared

- Check your history
- It may have been deleted
- Contact system administrator if concerned

### Manager hasn't reviewed yet

- Wait 5-7 business days
- Send a reminder to your manager
- Contact your HR department if delayed
